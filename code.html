About Us

Our Mission

At Digital Box, we believe in the power of disconnection to foster genuine human connection and personal growth. In today's hyper-connected world, we provide sanctuaries where you can step away from screens and reconnect with yourself, nature, and others.

Founded in 2020, our wellness retreats have helped thousands of urban professionals, students, and families find balance in their digital lives through carefully crafted programs that combine mindfulness, nature immersion, and holistic activities.
